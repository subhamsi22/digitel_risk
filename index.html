About IDR Section

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-focused initiative that prepares students and professionals for the rapidly evolving digital risk landscape.

academy

IDR Academy provides specialized training programs and bootcamps in digital risk, cyber security, and AI governance. Students and professionals gain practical skills that prepare them for real-world industry challenges.

Innovation & Incubation

IDR supports research and innovation in emerging digital risk areas. The institute encourages new ideas, startups, and technology solutions that address modern cyber and digital security challenges.

Advisory Services

IDR provides advisory services to organizations and institutions to help them manage digital risks effectively. This includes security frameworks, compliance strategies, and risk management solutions.
